About Us

Suffolk Security Services is a UK security company based in Ipswich, serving clients across Suffolk and nationwide.

We focus on delivering dependable security support with a professional, customer-first approach. Whether you need security for commercial premises, events, vacant properties or ongoing site support, we aim to provide a service that is efficient, visible and reassuring.
